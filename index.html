A. Bach Sofrox (pronounced "a box of rocks").

Typically, the phrase means "something of little value".
Music can be thought of that way; as a 
nearly-valueless commodity to be consumed
and disposed of in favor of something
fresh and new as dictated by time and style.

But for some of us, songs transcend the times that
made them popular. Good music provides an
unending soundtrack that enhances our every
thought and deed.

For that reason, I don't abandon the old
for the new.  I honor the great popular classics,
and blend them with some of the new to show
that good music is ageless.

My "box of rocks" is filled with gold and jewels;
hardly something of little value.

- John Clough, June 4, 2016
